Graphic Design

Graphic design is the art and practice of creating visual content to communicate messages or ideas to a target audience. It involves the use of typography, images, colors, shapes, and layouts to convey information effectively and aesthetically across various media, such as print, digital platforms, packaging, branding, and advertisements. Graphic design combines creativity, technical skills, and strategic thinking to produce visuals that not only capture attention but also fulfill a specific purpose, such as informing, persuading, or entertaining. It plays a key role in branding, marketing, and user experience.
